The Story Behind The Review Card

From a simple idea to a nationwide community of thriving local businesses.

The Review Card was born from a simple observation. Our founder, John S., noticed his favorite local spots—the family-run pizzerias, the friendly neighborhood dentists, the dedicated mechanics—were struggling to compete online against larger chains, not because of their quality, but because of their lack of reviews.

He saw that digital requests were often ignored, and in-person asks could be awkward. He knew there had to be a better way. That's when the idea for a simple, tangible card was born—a friendly, no-pressure reminder that customers could use on their own time. It started with one pizzeria, and the results were immediate and astounding. The rest, as they say, is history.
